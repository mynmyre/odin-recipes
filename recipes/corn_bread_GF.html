Gluten Free Corn Bread

You'd never guess this corn bread is gluten free!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar Or lemon juice
  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 1 cup millet flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200°c)
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl stir in milk, butter and vinegar(lemon).Stir cornmeal, millet flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in the egg mixture until combined; spread batter into the baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven 16 to 20 minutes until golden brown, cool for 10 minutes.

Gluten Free Artisan Bread

Healthy and easy GF bread you'll never miss regular bread again!

Ingredients

  • 300g gluten free flour
  • 280g luke warm water
  • 4g salt
  • 3g baker's yeast
  • 1 soup spoon of honey
  • 20g olive oil
  • 28cm iron tray

Steps

  1. Add the yeast to the water
  2. While mixing the flour add the yeast and water mixture incrementally
  3. Add the olive oil to the mix
  4. Put the batter in a oiled bowl and cover
  5. Make a ball
  6. Let it rise for 2h
  7. Remove it and slightly push to remove the gaz, make a burrito of it, repeat 2 times
  8. Put it upside down in another bowl and let it rise for 30 more minutes
  9. Cook for 15 minutes at 250Â°c

Gluten Free Chocolate Cake

Perfect chocolate cake and gluten free at that!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • vanilla (optional)
  • 100g sugar
  • 60ml vegetable oil
  • 130g (up to 150) GF flour
  • 2 soup spoon cocoa powder
  • 6g baking powder
  • 120ml milk
  • 1 butter spoon

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven at 180°c.
  2. Beat the eggs vanilla and sugar in a bowl for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the oil to the mix and beat again till the mixture is white.
  4. Add the flour, cocoa and baking powder over a strainer and add to the mix.
  5. Mix well with a spatula
  6. Heat the butter and milk together and add them while still hot to the mix.
  7. With a spatula mix the ingredients throughout.
  8. Put in a cooking tray and cook for 35 minutes at 180°c. cool for 10 minutes
